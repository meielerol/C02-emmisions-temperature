Climate Change: It's Impact on Earth

Climate change is one of the leading issues facing this planet. The rate of extinction is at an all time high, due to increased emissions of fossil fuels and the depletion of the ozone layer. In the past 100 years, 500 species have gone extinct due to changing circumstances in their habitat. While some have been able to surive, others have been unable to adapt to the drastic changes occuring on Earth.

We examine the changes in temperature and the increase emissions of fossil fuels as we try to find trends and correlations between average temperaturem, time and fossil fuel emissions.
